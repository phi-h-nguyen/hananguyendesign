COMMUNICATIONS AND OUTREACH ANNUAL MEETING 2016

The Communication and Outreach Department's 2016 annual meeting was held at the House of Blues and the theme was "Stop. Collaborate. Listen." I, along with other designers from my team, were pitted against each other in a fun and friendly "design-off" contest to design the event's t-shirt, notebook, lanyard, along with other promotional materials. My neon sign themed design won and will be used for all the event's needs.
