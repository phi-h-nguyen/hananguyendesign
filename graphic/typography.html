Below are a few of my favorite projects from my Typography course.

LEADERSHIP CONFERENCE
Previous Next

Every year, various Notre Dame clubs and groups come back to campus for three days of workshops and networking. The conference allows participants to network with other Notre Dame volunteer leaders from around the world, to hear campus updates directly from Notre Dame officials, and to develop skills that will specifically help in leadership roles. I created a concept for the logo, banner, and postcard for the event to correspond with this year's vintage theme.

STUDY BREAK

Every semester, the Alumni Association hosts an event during the week of exams for students. I created all graphics for this fun occasion, including posters, a Snapchat filter, Facebook banner, X banner, and other ads.
